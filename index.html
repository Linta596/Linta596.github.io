The Vet Clinic

Description

We cater to all types of pets ranging from cats and dogs to birds and bunnies. If your pet is sick or injured our clinic is the ideal place for you!

Information

Timings:

We are available for your pet all week! Daily timings are 12:00 - 22:00

IMPORTANT NOTE:

We are not equipped to cater to certain types of animals namely reptiles and amphibians. Our specialty is mammals. Hence, we are not willing to accept or provide treatment for snakes, frogs, crocodiles,etc.
